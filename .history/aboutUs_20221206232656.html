Our History

Future-Residency is a Burmese tech real-estate marketplace company that was founded in 2022, and was created by Mr. Pyae Sone Thant Kyaw, founder of burma-housing company Future-Residency has stated that it is a media company that generates revenue by selling advertising on its website. In April 2022, Future-Residency announced a partnership to lend its real-estate search engine to the websites of more than 180 burma newspapers as a part of the Future-Residency Newspaper Consortium. Future-Residency shares advertising revenue from the co-branded sites with the newspapers and extends its reach into local markets.
